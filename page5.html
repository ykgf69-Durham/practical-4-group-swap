Education and learning

There have been many advancements in techonology which have allowed the world to learn online. These advancements have created resources such as youtube, wikipedia, programming pages like W3Schools and many more.

Computers have made education more accessible for students of all backgrounds. Learners can access digital textbooks, online libraries, and educational tools from anywhere, which is especially helpful for those who may not have access to physical resources. Computers allow students to learn at their own pace and access support whenever they need it.
